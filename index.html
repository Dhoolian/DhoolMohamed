My Hobby: Beaded Jewellery Making

I have recently taken up beaded jewellery-making as a new hobby, and I am thoroughly enjoying learning the different techniques involved. What I especially love is how versatile this hobby is. I can create not only jewellery, but also bookmarks, belts, and decorative pieces using beads. I enjoy experimenting with different colour combinations and geometric patterns, constantly challenging myself to try new designs. Beading not only helps me relax but also strengthens my focus and patience. In time, I hope to continue developing my skills and eventually turn this hobby into a small online business.

Why I love This Hobby

  • It is versatile
  • Helps me relax and focus
  • I can create unique handmade gifts
  • Can turn it into a business
